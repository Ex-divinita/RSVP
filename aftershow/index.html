Tirana International Fair 30th Anniversary

UNITE, INVEST, INNOVATE: The AfterShow Report for a landmark event celebrating three decades of economic growth and regional collaboration in the Balkans.

2. Introduction

We are proud to present the aftershow report for the 30th anniversary edition of the Tirana International Fair, a milestone event celebrating three decades of fostering economic growth, innovation, and regional cooperation. This special edition featured the **30for30's Pavilion**, highlighting Albania's leading companies that have been operational for over 30 years.

The fair welcomed participation from 18 countries, reinforcing its role as a hub for international collaboration and commerce. Italy and Serbia stood out with the largest participation among foreign countries, contributing 35 and 25 companies, respectively. With 270 exhibitors and 21,285 attendees, this year's edition exemplified the Tirana International Fair's legacy as a bridge connecting Albania to the world.

14. Conclusions

The 30th edition of the Tirana International Fair stands as a landmark celebration of Albania's transformative journey, highlighting its rise as a dynamic hub of opportunity in the Balkans. Anchored by the theme "Unite, Invest, Innovate," the event set a new benchmark for success by fostering impactful connections across industries and championing sustainability and social responsibility.

The Fair laid the foundation for a sustainable and globally integrated future, solidifying its legacy as a beacon of collaboration, innovation, and transformative growth for Albania and its partners worldwide.
